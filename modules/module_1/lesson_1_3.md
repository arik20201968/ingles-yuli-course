## Telling the Time
In English, we usually ask: **"What time is it?"**
*   2:00 -> It's two o'clock.
*   2:15 -> It's two fifteen (or *a quarter past two*).
*   2:30 -> It's two thirty (or *half past two*).
*   2:45 -> It's two forty-five (or *a quarter to three*).
