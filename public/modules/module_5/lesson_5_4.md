# Lesson 5.4: The Weather and Seasons

## Introduction
Talking about the weather is very common in English conversations!

## The Weather
*   **Sunny** (Soleado)
*   **Cloudy** (Nublado)
*   **Rainy** (Lluvioso)
*   **Snowy** (Nevado)
*   **Hot** (Caluroso)
*   **Cold** (Frío)
*   **Windy** (Con viento)

## The Seasons
*   **Spring**: Primavera
*   **Summer**: Verano
*   **Autumn (Fall)**: Otoño
*   **Winter**: Invierno

## Weather Details
*   **It is hot and sunny**: Summer.
*   **It is cold and snowy**: Winter.
*   **It is rainy and windy**: Autumn.
*   **It is warm and cloudy**: Spring.
